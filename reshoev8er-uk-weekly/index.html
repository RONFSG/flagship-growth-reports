Weekly Insights & Actions
►
Weekly Highlights — Reshoev8er UK posted $1,013 in total sales across 25 units for Week 28, with PPC driving $502 of that revenue at a 1.90x ROAS. Wednesday July 8 was the peak sales day, suggesting mid-week demand remains the strongest window for this market. Overall performance reflects a stable baseline following the post-Prime Day cooldown period.
►
Metrics to Watch — The prior week included Amazon Prime Day (June 23–26, 2026), and the -70.9% WoW decline is expected post-event normalization, not a cause for alarm. CTR at 0.11% remains low and indicates ads are not compelling enough in the browse stage to drive meaningful click volume. CVR at 11.26% is a genuine bright spot, confirming that shoppers who do land on the listing are converting at a strong rate.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — ACOS at 52.5% is elevated relative to the 1.90x ROAS, signaling that PPC spend is consuming a disproportionate share of revenue and needs tightening. TACoS at 26.0% reflects that ad costs account for over a quarter of total sales, which is unsustainable at this revenue level without organic growth offsetting the burden. Reviewing underperforming keywords and pausing low-converting targets should be the immediate priority to bring ACOS closer to a 35–40% target range.
►
Actions This Week — Audit search term reports and pause any keywords with zero conversions over the past 14 days to reduce wasted spend pulling ACOS above 50%. Given the strong 11.26% CVR, consider increasing bids on top-converting keywords to capture more volume now that Prime Day traffic has settled. Run a mid-week promotional push on Wednesday to capitalize on the confirmed peak-day pattern and sustain momentum into Week 29.
