►

►
Metrics to Watch — ACOS at 28.1% and TACoS at 17.3% are areas we are actively refining as part of our ongoing campaign optimization process. Keyword bid adjustments and negative keyword expansion are already in motion to bring ACOS closer to our target range over the coming week. CTR at 0.26% presents a clear opportunity, and creative testing on ad copy and imagery is underway to lift click-through performance.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — With $992 deployed this week, we are focused on ensuring every dollar is allocated to the highest-performing placements and search terms. We are proactively reviewing campaign structure to shift budget toward converting keywords and reduce spend on lower-efficiency terms, which we expect to improve overall margin contribution. These adjustments are designed to protect our market position while driving more efficient growth heading into Week 30.
►
Actions This Week — We will refine keyword bids and expand negative keyword lists to bring ACOS down from 28.1% while maintaining strong sales volume and conversion rate. Creative assets will be refreshed and A/B tested to improve CTR beyond the current 0.26% baseline. We will also analyze Sunday July 12 peak-day data to identify any targeting or promotional patterns that can be replicated and sustained across additional days this week.
