Weekly Insights & Actions
►
Weekly Highlights — Reshoev8er Canada delivered a strong Week 28, generating $5,274 in total sales across 76 units sold, marking an 18.0% week-over-week increase. Tuesday July 7 stood out as the peak sales day, and a CVR of 12.60% confirms that shoppers who reached the listings were converting at a healthy rate.
►
Metrics to Watch — CTR at 0.29% remains the most pressing concern, indicating that ads are generating impressions but not compelling enough clicks to match the strong on-page conversion rate. Closing the gap between a 12.60% CVR and a 0.29% CTR represents the single largest untapped growth lever this week.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — The 3.86x ROAS and 25.9% ACoS reflect a functional but improvable paid traffic engine, with $927 in ad spend driving $3,576 in PPC revenue. TACoS at 17.6% is acceptable, though tightening ACoS toward the low 20s while maintaining volume would meaningfully improve overall profitability.
►
Actions This Week — Prioritize creative testing on ad imagery and headlines to address the 0.29% CTR, as even a modest improvement to 0.35–0.40% could significantly lift PPC revenue without increasing spend. Review Search Term Reports to identify high-spend, low-conversion keywords and reallocate that budget toward exact-match terms already contributing to the 12.60% CVR. Set a target ACoS ceiling of 23% for the coming week and pause or reduce bids on any campaigns trending above that threshold.
