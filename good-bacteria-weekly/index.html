►
Actions This Week — Audit and pause underperforming keywords contributing to the 103.6% ACOS, focusing budget on the top converting terms to reduce wasted spend. Test updated ad creative or main image to improve the 0.38% CTR and bring more qualified traffic at a lower cost-per-click. Set a target ACOS guardrail of 70% or below for the week and adjust bids daily to trend toward that threshold.
