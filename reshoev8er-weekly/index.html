Weekly Insights & Actions
►
Weekly Highlights — Reshoev8er generated $11,888 in total sales across 379 units for the week of July 6–12, 2026, with Tuesday July 7 standing out as the peak sales day. PPC drove $6,334 of total revenue at a strong 4.72x ROAS, reflecting solid return on paid investment. CVR came in at an impressive 18.65%, indicating that shoppers who engaged with the listings converted at a high rate.
►
Metrics to Watch — The prior week included Amazon Prime Day (July 8–9, 2026), and the -64.4% WoW decline is expected post-event normalization, not a cause for concern. TACoS at 11.4% and ACOS at 21.5% are worth monitoring as organic sales stabilize in the post-Prime Day period. CTR at 0.50% remains a recurring area to watch, as improving click-through rate would amplify the impact of the already strong 18.65% CVR.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — With $1,354 in ad spend generating $6,334 in PPC revenue, the 4.72x ROAS signals that current campaigns are performing efficiently. The 21.5% ACOS is acceptable but leaves room for tightening, particularly on any keywords that may have been bid up aggressively during Prime Day and have not been adjusted down. Reviewing search term reports this week to identify high-spend, low-conversion terms from the Prime Day window should be a priority.
►
Actions This Week — Audit all active PPC campaigns and reduce bids on keywords that surged during Prime Day but are no longer converting at target efficiency. Test 2–3 new ad creative variations or updated main images to push CTR above 0.60%, which at the current CVR would meaningfully increase total PPC revenue. Set a baseline sales target of $13,500–$15,000 for the coming week to begin rebuilding momentum toward pre-Prime Day volume levels.
