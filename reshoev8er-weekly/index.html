Weekly Insights & Actions
►
Weekly Highlights — Week 28 delivered strong momentum with $38,184 in total sales across 643 units, marking a 14.1% week-over-week increase. Tuesday, July 7 stood out as the peak sales day, anchoring a consistent mid-week performance surge. PPC drove $20,667 of total revenue, confirming paid traffic as a primary growth lever for Reshoev8er this period.
►
Metrics to Watch — CTR at 0.49% remains below the 0.5% threshold and signals that ad creative or targeting may not be capturing enough initial attention in the feed. CVR at 17.28% is a strong conversion signal, meaning the listing is closing well once shoppers arrive — the opportunity lies in driving more qualified clicks upstream. Continued monitoring of TACoS at 13.5% will help ensure total ad investment stays proportionate as sales volume scales.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — With $5,163 in ad spend generating a 4.00x ROAS and a 25.0% ACoS, paid performance is operating within a healthy and defensible range. TACoS at 13.5% confirms that PPC is contributing meaningfully to total revenue without overextending the ad budget relative to the full business. The gap between ACoS and TACoS suggests organic sales are providing a solid baseline, which creates room to test incremental budget on top-converting keywords without materially risking efficiency.
►
Actions This Week — Run a search term report to identify high-impression, low-CTR keywords and refresh ad copy or creative thumbnails to push CTR above 0.50%. Given the strong 17.28% CVR, increase bids on exact-match top performers to capture more volume while conversion rates remain elevated. Test a modest 10–15% ad spend increase on campaigns that drove Tuesday's peak-day performance to determine whether that mid-week demand pattern can be amplified and sustained.
