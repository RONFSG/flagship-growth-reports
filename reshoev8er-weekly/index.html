Weekly Insights & Actions
►
Weekly Highlights — Reshoev8er posted a strong Week 29 with $39,606 in total sales across 400 units sold, reflecting a healthy 5.8% week-over-week gain. Sunday July 12 drove peak volume, anchoring the week's momentum from the opening day. Conversion rate held firm at 15.22%, signaling that shoppers who found the listings were highly motivated to buy.
►
Metrics to Watch — CTR at 0.51% remains below typical benchmark thresholds, meaning a large portion of impressions are not translating into clicks and traffic generation is leaving value on the table. ACOS at 30.9% is worth monitoring closely, as it indicates that nearly a third of PPC revenue is being consumed by ad costs. Continued attention here will be important to ensure margin integrity as overall spend scales.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — The $3,843 in ad spend generated $12,440 in PPC revenue at a 3.24x ROAS, which is a solid return but leaves room for improvement given the 30.9% ACOS. TACoS at 9.7% reflects a reasonable ratio of ad spend to total sales, suggesting organic performance is carrying meaningful weight. Tightening bids on underperforming keywords could reduce ACOS while preserving the strong ROAS on top-converting terms.
►
Actions This Week — Audit low-CTR placements and refresh ad creative or main images to push CTR above 0.6%, targeting higher-quality clicks. Review keyword-level ACOS data and reduce bids or pause terms running above 35% to bring overall ACOS closer to 25%. Capitalize on the Sunday peak pattern by scheduling any promotions or deal pushes to launch Saturday night heading into the weekend.
