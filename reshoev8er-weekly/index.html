Weekly Insights & Actions
►
Weekly Highlights — Reshoev8er posted $39,606 in total sales across 720 units sold during Week 29, representing a solid 5.8% week-over-week increase. Sunday July 12 drove peak performance, anchoring a strong start to the week. PPC contributed $22,169 of total revenue, underscoring how central paid advertising remains to overall sales momentum.
►
Metrics to Watch — CTR at 0.56% remains below a healthy benchmark and signals that ad creatives or targeting may not be capturing shopper attention effectively. CVR at 15.58% is a genuine bright spot, meaning shoppers who do click are converting at a strong rate, so the priority is driving more qualified traffic to capitalize on that conversion strength. Monitoring the gap between ACOS at 30.0% and the blended TACoS at 16.8% will be important as ad efficiency is refined heading into Week 30.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — With $6,654 in ad spend generating a 3.33x ROAS, the account is performing efficiently, but ACOS at 30.0% leaves room for tightening bid strategies on underperforming keywords. Shifting budget toward top-converting ASINs and search terms could push ROAS above 3.5x while keeping TACoS at or below 16%. Reviewing search term reports for wasted spend on low-CVR queries should be an immediate priority this week.
►
Actions This Week — Audit all active campaigns for keywords with high spend but ACOS exceeding 35%, and either reduce bids or pause those terms to protect margin. Test updated ad creative or main images to improve CTR from 0.56% toward a 0.75% target, as even a modest lift would meaningfully increase traffic volume. Leverage the strong 15.58% CVR by increasing budget allocation to top-performing ad groups to scale revenue while maintaining current efficiency levels.
