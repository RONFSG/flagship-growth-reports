Weekly Insights & Actions
►
Weekly Highlights — Dormeo generated $122,099 in total sales across 321 units sold for the week of June 29 – July 5, with Sunday July 5 emerging as the peak sales day. PPC contributed $58,548 in revenue against $13,192 in ad spend, delivering a strong 4.65x ROAS. TACoS of 10.9% reflects healthy overall advertising efficiency relative to total revenue.
►
Metrics to Watch — The prior week included Amazon Prime Day (June 23–26, 2026), and the -53.1% WoW decline is expected post-event normalization, not a cause for concern. CTR of 0.44% and CVR of 2.17% are the metrics that warrant closer attention heading into the coming week, as both sit below benchmarks that would indicate strong listing and ad creative performance. Monitoring these conversion funnel metrics will be key to sustaining momentum after the Prime Day halo effect fades.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — ACOS of 23.9% is within a manageable range but leaves room for improvement, particularly by identifying underperforming keywords or placements that are driving spend without proportional returns. With $13,192 in ad spend generating $58,548 in PPC revenue, there is an opportunity to reallocate budget toward the highest-converting campaigns to push ACOS closer to the 18–20% target. Reviewing search term reports this week will help identify wasteful spend pulling the ACOS figure upward.
►
Actions This Week — Audit the top 20 PPC keywords by spend and pause or reduce bids on any converting below a 3x ROAS threshold to protect margin efficiency. Run a creative review on ad copy and main images to address the 0.44% CTR, testing a revised headline or lifestyle image to improve click volume. Capitalize on the Sunday peak-day pattern by scheduling any coupon activations or promotions to go live Saturday evening ahead of anticipated weekend traffic.
