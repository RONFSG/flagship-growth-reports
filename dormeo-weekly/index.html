Weekly Insights & Actions
►
Weekly Highlights — Dormeo closed Week 29 with $105,811 in total sales across 69 units sold, delivering a strong ROAS of 7.29x against $3,567 in ad spend. PPC revenue contributed $26,022, representing roughly 24.6% of total sales, with Saturday July 18 standing out as the peak revenue day. Overall performance remains healthy despite a modest -1.3% WoW dip.
►
Metrics to Watch — CTR of 0.35% is running below benchmark and suggests ad creative or keyword targeting may not be resonating strongly enough to drive clicks at scale. CVR at 2.73% is functional but leaves room for improvement, particularly given that stronger click quality could compound conversion gains. The -1.3% WoW decline is minor and does not signal a structural concern at this stage.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — With a TACoS of 3.4% and ACOS of 13.7%, the gap between total and PPC-attributed sales efficiency is notable and worth monitoring as organic contribution is doing significant heavy lifting. The 7.29x ROAS confirms that active ad dollars are being deployed efficiently, but the low CTR of 0.35% suggests spend may be reaching unqualified audiences or relying on underperforming creatives. Reallocating budget toward higher-converting placements could further tighten ACOS.
►
Actions This Week — Audit top-spend keywords for CTR performance and pause or replace any terms averaging below 0.3% CTR to improve click quality and reduce wasted impressions. Run an A/B test on primary ad creative or main image to address the CVR gap and push conversion rate closer to 3.5%+. Review Saturday July 18 traffic and order data to identify which products or placements drove the peak and replicate that exposure earlier in the week.
