Weekly Insights & Actions
►
Weekly Highlights — Dormeo closed Week 28 with $89,600 in total sales across 229 units sold, supported by a strong ROAS of 7.16x and a TACoS of 7.0%. PPC drove $42,461 in revenue, representing roughly 47% of total sales, with Tuesday July 7 standing out as the peak performance day. Overall ad efficiency remained healthy given the post-event demand environment.
►
Metrics to Watch — The prior week included Amazon Prime Day (June 23–26, 2026), and the -25.8% WoW decline is expected post-event normalization, not a cause for alarm. CTR at 0.51% and CVR at 2.64% are the metrics that warrant closer observation heading into the post-Prime Day cooldown period. Maintaining conversion rate above 2.5% will be important as traffic volumes naturally compress following peak event weeks.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — With $6,304 in ad spend generating $42,461 in PPC revenue, the campaign structure is performing efficiently at a 15.0% ACOS. There is room to evaluate whether budget is optimally distributed across top-performing ASINs versus catalog tail, particularly given the peak on Tuesday. Tightening bids on lower-converting keywords while protecting high-converting placements could incrementally improve ACOS below the 15% threshold.
►
Actions This Week — Review Tuesday July 7 traffic and conversion data to identify which campaigns or placements drove peak-day performance and replicate that structure. Audit keywords with CTR below 0.4% and pause or reduce bids to protect ad spend efficiency as organic velocity normalizes post-Prime Day. Set a weekly sales floor target of $85,000 for Week 29 and monitor daily pacing to catch any demand softness early enough to adjust bids proactively.
