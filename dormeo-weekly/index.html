Weekly Insights & Actions
►
Weekly Highlights — Dormeo closed Week 28 with $107,673 in total sales across 122 units sold, supported by a strong ROAS of 6.84x and a healthy TACoS of 6.7%. Sunday July 12 was the peak sales day, suggesting strong weekend momentum that can be built upon heading into Week 29. PPC drove $49,435 of total revenue, accounting for a significant portion of overall performance on a controlled ad spend of $7,226.
►
Metrics to Watch — The week-over-week decline of -10.9% warrants monitoring, though total sales remaining above $107K indicates the business is performing from a solid baseline. CTR at 0.53% and CVR at 2.59% are areas to observe closely, as improvements in either metric would have a compounding effect on PPC revenue and overall efficiency. ACOS at 14.6% is acceptable but leaves room for tightening as campaigns are optimized.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — With $7,226 in ad spend generating $49,435 in PPC revenue, the 6.84x ROAS reflects strong campaign efficiency worth protecting. The 14.6% ACOS suggests there may be underperforming keywords or placements absorbing spend without proportional return, and a targeted audit could recover margin. Prioritizing high-converting ad placements that align with the Sunday peak-day pattern could further improve spend allocation.
►
Actions This Week — Conduct a keyword-level ACOS audit to identify terms exceeding 20% ACOS and reallocate that budget toward top converters to bring overall ACOS below 13%. Run a CTR improvement test on primary listings by refreshing main images or headline copy, targeting a CTR lift toward 0.65% or higher. Leverage Sunday's peak-day data to front-load weekend ad budgets and capture demand during the highest-converting window of the week.
