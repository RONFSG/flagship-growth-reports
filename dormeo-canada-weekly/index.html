►

There is room to test modest bid increases on top-converting keywords to capture incremental share without meaningfully disrupting efficiency ratios.

Actions This Week — Run a search term audit on Week 28 PPC data to identify any wasted spend on low-converting queries and redirect that budget toward proven performers.
