►

►

►

This level of efficiency suggests there may be headroom to scale spend without meaningfully eroding margins. Testing a controlled budget increase of 15–20% on top-performing campaigns could capture additional revenue while staying well within acceptable ACoS thresholds.
►
Actions This Week — Audit creative assets across top PPC campaigns to identify opportunities to improve CTR from 0.39% toward 0.5% or higher through stronger imagery or headline copy. Leverage Friday's peak-day data to assess whether dayparting or bid adjustments on Thursdays and Fridays could amplify that momentum. Review the 106-unit sales mix to confirm inventory depth is sufficient to support a potential spend scale-up without risking stockouts.
