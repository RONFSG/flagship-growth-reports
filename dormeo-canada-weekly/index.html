Weekly Insights & Actions
►
Weekly Highlights — Dormeo Canada closed Week 28 with $66,974 in total sales across 90 units, supported by a strong ROAS of 10.91x and a TACoS of just 6.3%. PPC contributed $45,730 of total revenue, demonstrating that paid traffic remains the primary growth engine. Sunday, July 12 was the peak sales day, suggesting end-of-week momentum worth monitoring going forward.
►
Metrics to Watch — Total sales declined 18.9% week over week, which warrants a closer look at demand trends heading into Week 29. CTR of 0.33% remains below healthy benchmarks and indicates that ad creative or keyword targeting may not be capturing shopper attention effectively. CVR at 2.65% is functional but leaves room for improvement, particularly if listing content or pricing can be refined to convert more of the existing traffic.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — With $4,193 in ad spend generating $45,730 in PPC revenue, the account is operating efficiently at a 9.2% ACoS. However, the gap between PPC revenue ($45,730) and total sales ($66,974) suggests a meaningful share of organic revenue that should be protected by maintaining ad visibility on top-performing ASINs. Monitoring spend allocation by day of week could also help capitalize on the Sunday peak pattern observed this week.
►
Actions This Week — Review and test updated ad creatives or headline copy to address the low 0.33% CTR, prioritizing Sponsored Brand and Sponsored Display placements. Audit the top-converting keywords driving PPC revenue to ensure bids are competitive and budgets are not capping out during peak hours. Conduct a listing audit on the highest-traffic ASINs to identify any content gaps in images, bullets, or pricing that may be suppressing the 2.65% CVR.
