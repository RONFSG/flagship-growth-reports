Weekly Insights & Actions
►
Weekly Highlights — Hapad delivered $16,418 in total sales across Week 29, with PPC driving $9,551 of that revenue and a strong conversion rate of 17.73% reflecting highly relevant traffic and solid product-market fit. Friday July 17 stood out as the peak sales day, anchoring a productive mid-week stretch. With 1,126 units moved and a ROAS of 2.16x, the campaigns continued to generate meaningful returns on ad investment.
►
Metrics to Watch — ACOS at 46.3% and TACoS at 26.9% are areas we are actively optimizing as part of our ongoing campaign refinement process. The WoW dip of 7.4% is being monitored in context, and our focus this week is on tightening keyword targeting and reallocating budget toward the highest-converting placements. As these adjustments take hold, we expect both efficiency metrics to trend in a more favorable direction.
►
Ad Spend Efficiency — At $4,419 in ad spend against $16,418 in total sales, we are proactively working to improve the spend-to-revenue ratio while maintaining visibility in a competitive category. Our approach this week centers on reducing investment in lower-performing ad groups and reinforcing spend where CTR and CVR are strongest, particularly given the 17.73% conversion rate signaling strong intent from engaged shoppers. These targeted adjustments are designed to protect market position while improving overall margin contribution.
►
Actions This Week — We will audit current keyword bids to reduce spend on terms driving high clicks but lower conversion, while scaling budget toward placements that are contributing to the strong 17.73% CVR. CTR at 0.57% presents a clear opportunity to test refreshed ad creative and titles to improve click-through performance. We will also review day-parting data to determine whether concentrating spend around Friday patterns can help replicate and build on the peak-day performance seen on July 17.
